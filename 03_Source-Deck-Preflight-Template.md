## 9. Two Prompts / Actions (slides 8 + 9)

The reader needs to leave with something they can do. Two prompts:

**Slide 8 — Tactical / immediate win:**
- Prompt: `[A specific, low-effort action a reader can try this week]`
- Insight: `[What they'll see when they do it]`

**Slide 9 — Stretch / strategic:**
- Prompt: `[A bigger, more thoughtful application]`
- Insight: `[The meta-insight that emerges]`

> **Differentiate by ask, not visual.** Both slides look the same — the *content* must clearly distinguish tactical from strategic.
